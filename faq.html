Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?
Reach out to us through our Contact Us page to begin the order process. We can provide responses to any questions you may have and will do our best to respond to you within 24 hours. We typically require a PO and proof of funds to initiate an order. Shipping information will be promptly sent to you as we receive it.

What are the payment terms?
We are flexible on this but we prefer 100% payment upfront prior to shipment. We accept wire transfers.

Can you provide samples?
We currently only have samples of the Makrite 9500-N95 Mask and Cobes Level 3 Surgical Gown. We will try to get more samples over time.
